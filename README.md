# AI-Powered Job Application Agent — Project Management Case Study

**Course:** Project Management
---

## Overview

This repository documents the end-to-end project management of an AI-Powered Job Application Agent — a SaaS platform designed to automate resume tailoring and job application submission on behalf of users. The project was planned and managed following industry-standard PM methodologies, covering scope definition, scheduling, budgeting, risk management, stakeholder communication, and quality assurance.

---

## Project Objective

To deliver a working SaaS platform by June 30, 2026 that enables an AI Agent to apply for jobs across at least 3 major job boards, with AI-driven resume and cover letter tailoring validated by pilot users.

## Make-Buy Decision

For Release 5 (Compliance & Privacy), an internal development approach was selected over third-party consultants. The cost to build internally was $20,500 versus $57,500 to buy, while also building long-term in-house expertise.

---

## Key QA Finding

Initial testing of Release 2 identified two critical issues: the AI model required deeper resume content personalization, and the UI editor needed to be rebuilt as a modern interactive experience. A rework was prioritized before proceeding to Release 3.

---

## SWOT Summary

**Strength:** Intuitive, user-friendly onboarding experience that is a strong market differentiator.  
**Weakness:** High dependency on the stability and availability of external job board APIs.  
**Opportunity:** Licensing the platform to university career centers and coding bootcamps.  
**Threat:** Evolving anti-bot protocols on job boards, mitigated through a human-in-the-loop review design.
